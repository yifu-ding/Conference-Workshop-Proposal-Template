\subsection{Submission and Review Process}

\begin{description}[style=unboxed, leftmargin=0pt, itemsep=6pt]
    \item[\textbf{Submission Types:}] We accept two tracks of submissions using the official \conf style template: (1) Full Papers: Up to 8 pages (excluding references). (2)
    Extended Abstracts: Up to 4 pages (including references).

    \item[\textbf{Review Policy:}] All submissions will undergo a \textbf{double-blind} peer-review process by at least three program committee members. Accepted works will be assigned as either a Contributed Talk (Oral) or a Poster Presentation.

    \item[\textbf{Review Timeline:}] The schedule is strictly aligned with \conf universal deadlines:
