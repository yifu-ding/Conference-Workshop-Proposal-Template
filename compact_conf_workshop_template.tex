\subsection{Submission and Review Process}
\noindent \textbf{Tracks \& Review:} We accept \textbf{Full Papers} (up to 8 pages, excluding references) and \textbf{Extended Abstracts} (up to 4 pages, including references). All submissions will undergo a \textbf{double-blind} peer-review process by at least three experts. Accepted works will be assigned as either a Contributed Talk (Oral) or a Poster.
